Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Add butter or oil over medium heat.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour eggs and spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Add fillings when eggs start setting.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold and cook for another minute.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot.
